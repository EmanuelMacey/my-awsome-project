### 1. Browse Stores
- Open the app to see all available stores
- Filter by category: Fast Food, Supermarket, Pharmacy
- Search for specific stores or products
- Tap any store to view its menu

### 2. View Starbucks Menu
- Find "Starbucks" in the store list
- Choose your preferred location
- Browse by category:
  - Hot Beverages
  - Cold Beverages
  - Food
- All items show real product images

### 3. Add Items to Cart
- Tap any product card
- Click "Add to Cart" button
- Choose to continue shopping or view cart
- Cart badge shows total items

### 4. Checkout
- Tap "View Cart" button
- Review your items
- Select payment method:
  - Visa/Mastercard
  - MMG+
  - Cash on Delivery
- Confirm your order

### 5. Track Your Order
- View order status in "My Orders"
- Track delivery in real-time
- Chat with your driver
- Rate your experience
