
# Shopping & Pharmacy Errands - User Guide

## Overview
ErrandRunners now handles supermarket shopping and pharmacy pickups through our convenient errand service. This guide explains how to use these services.

## üõçÔ∏è Supermarket Shopping

### How to Order

1. **Open the App**
   - Launch ErrandRunners
   - You'll see the home screen with fast food restaurants

2. **Access Errands**
   - Tap the blue "Need an Errand?" button at the top
   - Or navigate to the Errands tab

3. **Select Shopping Errands**
   - Tap on "Shopping Errands" category
   - Select "Supermarket Shopping"

4. **Provide Details**
   - **Pickup Address:** Enter the supermarket name and location
     - Example: "Massy Stores MovieTowne"
     - Example: "Bounty Supermarket Sheriff Street"
   - **Drop-off Address:** Enter your delivery address
     - Example: "123 Main Street, Georgetown"

5. **Shopping List**
   - In the "Instructions" field, provide a detailed shopping list
   - Include quantities and brands
   - Example:
     ```
     - 2 lbs chicken breast
     - 1 gallon whole milk (Demerara brand)
     - 1 loaf whole wheat bread
     - 6 eggs (large)
     - 2 lbs rice (Karibee brand)
     - 1 bottle cooking oil (750ml)
     ```

6. **Additional Notes**
   - Add any special instructions
   - Example: "If chicken breast not available, get chicken thighs"
   - Example: "Call me if total exceeds $5,000"

7. **Choose Payment Method**
   - Cash
   - Card
   - MMG+
   - Bank Transfer

8. **Confirm**
   - Review your errand details
   - Fixed errand fee: **GYD $2,000**
   - Tap "Confirm & Submit"

### What Happens Next

1. **Runner Assignment**
   - A verified runner (Emanuel Macey) will be assigned
   - You'll receive a notification

2. **Shopping**
   - Runner goes to the specified supermarket
   - Purchases items from your list
   - May contact you for clarifications

3. **Payment for Items**
   - Runner pays for items upfront
   - You reimburse runner upon delivery
   - Or runner can use your payment method if arranged

4. **Delivery**
   - Runner delivers items to your address
   - You pay the GYD $2,000 errand fee
   - Plus reimburse for item costs

‚úÖ **Be Specific**
- Include exact quantities
- Specify brands when important
- Mention size/weight preferences

‚úÖ **Provide Alternatives**
- "If X brand not available, get Y brand"
- "If item out of stock, call me"

‚úÖ **Set Budget**
- Mention if you have a budget limit
- "Please keep total under $10,000"

‚úÖ **Contact Info**
- Ensure your phone number is correct
- Runner may need to call for clarifications

## üíä Pharmacy Errands

### How to Order

1. **Open the App**
   - Launch ErrandRunners
   - Tap "Need an Errand?" button

2. **Select Medical Errands**
   - Tap on "Medical Errands" category
   - Select "Pharmacy Errands"

3. **Provide Details**
   - **Pickup Address:** Enter pharmacy name and location
     - Example: "Mike's Pharmacy Water Street"
     - Example: "Smart Aid 24-Hour Pharmacy Sheriff Street"
   - **Drop-off Address:** Enter your delivery address

4. **Prescription/Item Details**
   - In "Instructions" field, provide:
     - Prescription details
     - Or list of over-the-counter items needed
   - Example:
     ```
     Prescription for John Doe
     - Amoxicillin 500mg (as prescribed)
     - Paracetamol 500mg (1 box)
     - Cough syrup (any brand)
     ```

5. **Upload Documents** (if needed)
   - Upload prescription photo
   - Upload authorization letter if picking up for someone else

6. **Additional Notes**
   - Any special instructions
   - Example: "Ask pharmacist for generic alternative if available"

7. **Choose Payment Method**
   - Select how you'll pay

8. **Confirm**
   - Review details
   - Fixed errand fee: **GYD $2,000**
   - Submit errand

### What Happens Next

1. **Runner Assignment**
   - Verified runner assigned
   - Notification sent to you

2. **Pharmacy Visit**
   - Runner goes to specified pharmacy
   - Presents prescription if applicable
   - Purchases items

3. **Delivery**
   - Runner delivers to your address
   - You pay GYD $2,000 errand fee
   - Plus reimburse for medication costs

### Important Notes

‚ö†Ô∏è **Prescriptions**

- Ensure prescription is valid

‚ö†Ô∏è **Authorization**

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I choose which supermarket?
Yes! Specify the exact supermarket in the pickup address field.

### What if an item is out of stock?
Add instructions like "Call me if item not available" or "Get alternative brand if needed."

### How do I pay for the items?
Runner pays upfront. You reimburse upon delivery, or you can arrange to provide payment method in advance.

### Can I order from multiple stores?
Each errand is for one store. Create separate errands for multiple stores.

### What's the delivery time?
- Supermarket shopping: 1-2 hours
- Pharmacy errands: 30-45 minutes
- Times may vary based on store location and traffic

### Can I schedule for later?
Yes! Choose "Schedule for Later" and select your preferred date and time.

### What if I need to cancel?
Contact support immediately. Cancellation policy applies if runner has already started shopping.

### Is there a minimum order?
No minimum! Order as little or as much as you need.

### Can I tip the runner?
Yes! Tips are appreciated but not required. You can tip in cash upon delivery.
