
# Cash on Delivery (COD) - User Guide

## Overview

ErrandRunners now uses **Cash on Delivery (COD)** as the primary payment method. This means you pay for your order or errand with cash when it's delivered to you.

## How It Works

### For Food Orders:

1. **Browse & Add to Cart**
   - Browse restaurants and add items to your cart
   - View your cart to see the total amount

2. **Checkout**
   - Tap "Proceed to Checkout"
   - Pin your delivery location on the map
   - Review your order summary
   - Payment method is automatically set to "Cash on Delivery"

3. **Place Order**
   - Tap "Confirm Order"
   - You'll receive a digital receipt
   - Track your order status in real-time

4. **Delivery & Payment**
   - Driver will deliver your order
   - Pay the exact amount in cash to the driver
   - Enjoy your meal!

### For Errands:

1. **Select Errand Type**
   - Choose from categories like Shopping, Document Delivery, etc.
   - Select specific service

2. **Provide Details**
   - Enter pickup and drop-off locations
   - Add instructions and notes
   - Upload any necessary documents

3. **Schedule**
   - Choose ASAP or schedule for later

4. **Confirm & Submit**
   - Review errand summary
   - Payment method is automatically set to "Cash on Delivery"
   - Submit your errand request

5. **Completion & Payment**
   - Runner completes your errand
   - Pay the total amount in cash to the runner
   - Receive your items/documents

## Pricing

### Food Delivery:
- **Subtotal**: Cost of all items
- **Service Fee**: GYD$200 (fast food only)
- **Delivery Fee**: GYD$1,200 (fast food) or GYD$2,000 (other)
- **Total**: Sum of all charges

### Errands:
- **Base Price**: GYD$2,000
- **Distance Fee**: Varies based on distance
- **Complexity Fee**: Varies based on task complexity
- **Total**: Sum of all charges

## Tips for Smooth Transactions

### Prepare Exact Change:
- Have the exact amount ready when the driver/runner arrives
- This speeds up the transaction and avoids delays

### Check Your Receipt:
- Review the digital receipt before delivery
- Know the exact amount you need to pay

### Verify Your Order:
- Check your order/errand items before paying
- Ensure everything is correct

### Keep Your Phone Handy:
- Driver/runner may call if they can't find your location
- Track delivery status in real-time

## Digital Receipts

After placing an order or errand, you'll receive a digital receipt showing:

- Order/Errand number
- Date and time
- Items or service details
- Price breakdown
- Delivery address
- Payment method (Cash on Delivery)
- Support contact information

You can access your receipts anytime from:
- Order history (for food orders)
- My Errands (for errand services)

### Common Questions:

**Q: Can I pay with card or mobile money?**
A: Currently, only Cash on Delivery is available. Additional payment methods will be added soon.

**Q: What if I don't have exact change?**
A: Drivers/runners will try to provide change, but having exact amount is appreciated.

**Q: Can I cancel after placing an order?**
A: Yes, you can cancel before the driver picks up your order. Contact support for assistance.

**Q: Is there a minimum order amount?**
A: Minimum order amounts vary by restaurant. Check the restaurant details.

**Q: Are there any additional fees?**
A: All fees are shown in the order summary before you confirm. No hidden charges.

## Safety Tips

- Meet the driver/runner in a safe, well-lit location
- Verify the order/errand details before accepting
- Count your change if applicable
- Report any issues immediately to support
