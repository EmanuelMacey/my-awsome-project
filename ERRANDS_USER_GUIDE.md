### Tracking Your Errand

**Status Timeline:**
1. **Pending** - Waiting for runner assignment
2. **Accepted** - Runner has accepted your errand
3. **At Pickup** - Runner is at the pickup location
4. **Pickup Complete** - Runner has collected the item/document
5. **En Route** - Runner is on the way to drop-off
6. **Completed** - Errand successfully completed

**What You Can Do:**
- View real-time status updates
- See runner information (name, phone)
- Call the runner directly
- View price breakdown
- Cancel errand (if still pending)

### Viewing Your Errands

**My Errands Screen:**
- **Active Tab** - Shows ongoing errands (pending, accepted, in progress)
- **Completed Tab** - Shows finished errands (completed, cancelled)

**Each Errand Card Shows:**
- Errand number (e.g., #ERR1234567890)
- Category and subcategory
- Current status with color coding
- Pickup and drop-off addresses
- Total price
- Date created

## For Runners

### Accepting Errands

1. **View Available Errands**
   - Browse pending errands in your area
   - See pickup and drop-off locations
   - Check payment method and price

2. **Accept Errand**
   - Tap "Accept" to take the errand
   - View customer details
   - See uploaded documents (if any)

### Updating Errand Status

**Status Flow:**
1. Tap "At Pickup" when you arrive at pickup location
2. Tap "Pickup Complete" after collecting item/document
3. Tap "En Route" when heading to drop-off
4. Tap "Complete" when delivered

**Important:**
- Update status promptly for customer visibility
- Each status change is timestamped
- Customer receives real-time notifications

### Viewing Documents

**Security:**
- Only you (assigned runner) can view uploaded documents
- Documents include authorization letters, receipts, IDs
- Handle sensitive documents with care

## Pricing

### Price Breakdown:
- **Base Price** - Service fee for the errand type
- **Distance Fee** - GYD $200 per kilometer
- **Complexity Fee** - Additional fee for complex tasks
  - Low: $0
  - Medium: $500
  - High: $1,000

### Example Prices:
- GRA License Pickup: GYD $1,500
- NIS Form Drop-off: GYD $1,200
- DHL Package Pickup: GYD $1,800
- Bill Payment: GYD $800
- Custom Errand: Varies

## Tips for Success

### For Customers:
1. **Be Specific** - Provide clear pickup and drop-off addresses
2. **Add Instructions** - Include any special requirements
3. **Upload Documents** - Attach authorization letters when needed
4. **Choose Right Time** - Select ASAP or schedule for later
5. **Verify Details** - Double-check all information before confirming

### For Runners:
1. **Communicate** - Call customer if you have questions
2. **Update Status** - Keep customer informed of progress
3. **Handle with Care** - Treat documents and items carefully
4. **Be Punctual** - Arrive on time at pickup and drop-off
5. **Confirm Completion** - Verify delivery before marking complete

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does an errand take?**
A: Most errands are completed within 1-3 hours, depending on distance and complexity.

**Q: Can I cancel an errand?**
A: Yes, you can cancel pending errands. Once accepted by a runner, contact support.

**Q: What if I need to upload more documents?**
A: You can upload multiple documents when creating the errand.

**Q: How do I know my documents are secure?**
A: Documents are encrypted, access-controlled, and auto-deleted after 30 days.

**Q: Can I track the runner in real-time?**
A: Status updates are provided at each stage. Map tracking coming soon.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: MMG, Visa/Mastercard, and Cash on Delivery.

**Q: How is the price calculated?**
A: Base price + distance fee + complexity fee = total price.

**Q: Can I schedule an errand for later?**
A: Yes, you can choose ASAP or schedule for a specific time.
